There is still some planning as to how this will work, but here is a rough idea: The site will allow users that login to rank each other based on how canonically heavy they are (aka, how big and fat their character is), a point system will be devised where people will be able to rate the "chunkiness" of someone else's character on scale (perhaps from 1 -100) and based on how many people join will determine the number of slots available for each tier. Again, this is a work in progress and who knows, perhaps this site will change into something totally different from what it's planned out to be. Stay tuned.
